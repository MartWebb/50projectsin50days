My Pet Turtle

Ever since watching the Teenage Mutant Hero Turtles when I was 8 I wanted a real turtle. One day I was just walking in the sewer, as you do, and I spotted a turtle. I said to myself "he is the perfect pet for an apartment", so I took him home. Tim the turtle has been with me 33 years now, and he is my best friend, I keep him in the bath of course. It gets a bit cramped when me or my wife have a bath or shower, but I would not have it any other way.
